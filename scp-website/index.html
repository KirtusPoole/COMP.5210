Welcome to the SCP Foundation

The SCP Foundation is dedicated to the protection of humanity from anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. Our mission is to secure, contain, and protect.

About SCPs

SCPs (Secure, Contain, Protect) are anomalous items, creatures, or places that defy natural laws. Our researchers work tirelessly to understand and contain these anomalies to ensure global safety.
